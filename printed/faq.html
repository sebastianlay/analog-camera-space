overview / frequently asked questions

  • Why isn’t camera x listed on this site?

    This site attempts to list all analog still cameras that can be 3D printed by everyone.

    You can request the addition of a camera in case I missed one!

    Request Camera

  • What about films that are needed for these cameras?

    I’m glad you asked! I have also created a sister site to this one where I attempt to list all analog films that are still in production and are widely available.

    You can check it out here:

    analogfilm.space
